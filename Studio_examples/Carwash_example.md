# Carwash business example

I have a small carwash business.  I sell services such as Basic_Wash, Super_wash, and Detailing. I want only my most senior employees to perform the detailing service.  My newest employees can perform the Basic_wash service, only trusted employees can operate the cash register or open up the business.  Bob is my most senior and trusted employee. I just hired Ted. and Alice is the store manager and their boss.
